What equipment is guarding the Ukrainian sky

Currently, the Ukrainian anti-aircraft missile forces (not including parts of the army air defense) are equipped with the following equipment:

S-300PT/PS anti-aircraft missile system

Maximum damage range – 75 km (aerodynamic target) 30 km (ballistic target)

The maximum height of the lesion is 27 km

The minimum height of the lesion is 0.025 km

The probability of hitting a fighter-type target with one missile is 0.8-0.9

The probability of hitting a cruise missile target is 0.4-0.8

A total of about 28 S-300PT/PS divisions are in service. Each division has up to 12 launchers with four missiles. A total of 48 missiles in the first salvo per missile division.

S-300PT/PS anti-aircraft missile system

S-300V anti-aircraft missile system

Maximum damage range – 100 km (aerodynamic target) 40 km (ballistic target)

The maximum height of the lesion is 30 km

The minimum height of the lesion is 0.025 km

The probability of hitting a fighter-type target with one missile is 0.7-0.9

The probability of hitting a cruise missile target is 0.5-0.7

In total, about 3 S-300B divisions are in service. Each division has up to 12 launchers with four missiles. A total of 48 missiles in the first salvo per missile division.

S-300V anti-aircraft missile system

BUK-M1 anti-aircraft missile complex

Maximum damage range – 35 km (aerodynamic target) 25 km (ballistic target)

The maximum height of the lesion is 22 km

The minimum height of the lesion is 0.015 km

The probability of hitting a fighter-type target with one missile is 0.8-0.95

The probability of hitting a cruise missile target is 0.4-0.6

A total of about 10 BUK-M1 divisions are in service. Each division has up to 6 launchers with four missiles. A total of 24 missiles in the first salvo per missile division.

BUK-M1 anti-aircraft missile complex

Anti-aircraft missile complex S-125-2D1

The maximum damage range is 35 km

The maximum height of the lesion is 21 km

A total of 1 S-125-2D1 division is in service. The division has 4 launchers with four missiles. A total of 16 missiles in the first salvo per division.

Locations of missile divisions (unmarked, in the center of the circles) and, accordingly, areas of cover by anti-aircraft missile troops of the territory of Ukraine are shown on the map. Small rounds - BUK-M1 and S-125-2D1, medium rounds S-300PT/PS, large round S-300B. One circle is one division. The map is designed according to the locations of the divisions.
